Snowball

Status:

Permanent Cat

Gender:

Female

DOB:

2025-06-05

Snowball is a sweet, affectionate young kitten with a heart as soft as her fur. She greets everyone with bright eyes and a gentle purr the moment they walk into the room, making it impossible not to fall for her.

Cuddles are Snowball’s love language. She happily curls up in any available lap, soaking up affection like sunshine. Endearingly needy in the best way, she follows her favorite people around, offering tiny mews as a reminder that it’s time for more attention.

Playful, loving, and full of charm, Snowball brings warmth and joy into every space she enters — the perfect companion for anyone who loves constant kitten affection and closeness.
